The process

Simple, gentle,
and completely free.

01

Tell us about your situation

Answer a few gentle questions about your loved one, your location, and your budget. No wrong answers, no commitment. Takes about two minutes.

02

We find your best matches

We identify the providers that are the best fit for your family — based on your needs, not on who pays us the most.

03

Pricing, distance, availability — sent directly to your inbox. No sales calls, no pressure.

Contact any provider when you're ready. You're never under any obligation, and we're here if you have questions.

About the Founder

My name is John, a computer science graduate of USC. When my beloved granddad passed away, our family was scammed out of thousands of dollars by a fraudulent funeral home. That experience never left me, and it's the reason I built Resting Place.

Our Standards

Any establishment with a pattern of low reviews, unexpected upcharges, unexplained "security deposits," or other predatory practices is permanently blacklisted and will never appear in your results — regardless of what they offer to pay for placement.
