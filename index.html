The process

Simple, gentle,
and completely free.

From your first question to your final decision, we're here to make one of the hardest tasks a little easier.

01

Tell us about your situation

Answer a few gentle questions about your loved one, your preferences, your location, and your budget. There are no wrong answers and no commitment required. The whole process takes about two minutes.

02

We find your best matches

Based on your answers, we identify the two or three providers that are the best fit for your family — not the ones that pay us the most. Every option has been personally reviewed for quality, transparency, and fair pricing before it ever appears in your results.

03

You receive a personal match report

Your results are sent directly to your email, along with pricing information, distance, availability, and a note about why each provider was selected for you specifically. No sales calls. No pressure. Just clear, honest information.

04

Reach out on your own terms

When you're ready, contact any provider directly. We never share your information without your knowledge, and you are never under any obligation. If you have more questions, simply reply to your email — we're here for as long as you need us.

Our story

Built for families,
not for profit.

Resting Place was created out of a simple belief: that no family should have to navigate one of life's hardest decisions alone — or be taken advantage of while doing it.

About the Founder

My name is John, and I'm a computer science student at the University of Southern California. I built Resting Place because I believe the funeral and burial industry — despite serving families at their most vulnerable — has long operated with a troubling lack of transparency and accountability.

This is a non-profit service. I didn't create it to generate revenue, but to give families a reliable, compassionate resource at a moment when they have very little time, energy, or emotional bandwidth to research their options on their own. Every match sent through this platform is filtered through a rigorous vetting process before it ever reaches you.

Our Standards

Not every cemetery or funeral home makes the cut — and that's entirely by design. Every provider is put through a careful review process examining customer feedback, pricing transparency, and overall business practices.

Any establishment with a pattern of low reviews, unexpected upcharges, unexplained "security deposits," or other predatory practices is permanently blacklisted and will never appear in your results — no matter how much they offer to pay for placement. Your trust is worth more than any referral fee.
